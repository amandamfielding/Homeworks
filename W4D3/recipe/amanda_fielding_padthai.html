Amanda Fielding Recipe

Pad Thai

Description

Homemade Pad Thai noodles with shrimp, tofu, peanuts in savory sweet sauce. The best and easiest Pad Thai recipe ever! Serves 2 Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 oz packaged rice noodles
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 clove garlic, finely minced
  • 4 oz medium-sized shrimp, shelled and deveined
  • 1 large egg
  • 6 oz bean sprouts
  • 2 tbls crushed peanuts
  • lime wedges

Directions
  1. boil noodles
  2. add oil to heated skillet
  3. add garlic
  4. add shrimp
  5. add noodles
  6. push ingredients aside and add egg to empty section
  7. combine with sauces, sprouts, chives and peanuts
  8. serve with lime wedges
